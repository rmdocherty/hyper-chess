About:

Hyperchess is a platform for creating and playing non-Euclidean chess games. In this context, non-Euclidean refers to loops connecting same sides of a board or lines connecting opposite sides of the board. Users are encouraged to create their own boards via the 'BUILD' button and to play with friends over a peer-to-peer connection with the 'PLAY' button.

Rules:

  • No checks - take the King to win
  • Auto-promote to Queen only
  • Everything else (castling, en passant) as in regular chess
